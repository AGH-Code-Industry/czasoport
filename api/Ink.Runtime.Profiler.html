Class Profiler

Namespace
Ink.Runtime

Simple ink profiler that logs every instruction in the story and counts frequency and timing. To use:

var profiler = story.StartProfiling(),

(play your story for a bit)

var reportStr = profiler.Report();

story.EndProfiling();
